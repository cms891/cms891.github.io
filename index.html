Our Mission

The Association of Commuter Students is dedicated to supporting and advocating for students who commute to campus. We provide resources, social events, and a central hub to ensure every commuter has a positive and inclusive university experience.

Learn more about our goals and current leadership team.

Why Join the ACS?

Community & Networking

  • Meet and connect with fellow commuters who share similar experiences.
  • Expand your campus network beyond your specific classes or major.

Resources & Support

  • Access to valuable resources like study spaces, parking tips, and local transit information.
  • Participate in workshops focused on time management and commuter life balance.
